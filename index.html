Why it matters

A quiet channel reads as a quiet school.

Perception drives enrolment — and enrolment shapes funding, staffing, and community confidence. Your social media is doing the talking whether you tend it or not.

i.

The first place families look

Long before the open day or the enrolment form, parents look you up online. The feed is the first impression — often the only one.

ii.

Silence sends a signal

A neglected channel reads as a neglected school. An active, responsive one signals a thriving, well-run community.

iii.

Perception drives enrolment

Confidence compounds. How the school looks online shapes who chooses to walk through the gate — and stay.

iv.

Without adding to the workload

Teachers and office staff are already stretched. We carry the channel so the school doesn't have to.

The scope of work

Everything the channel needs — handled.

01

Content strategy & planning

A calendar that thinks ahead — mapped to your term rhythm and the moments that matter most for enrolment.

  • Content calendar mapped to the school term, key events, and enrolment periods
  • Content pillars and messaging tailored to your brand, voice, and community
  • Channel strategy across the platforms that matter — Facebook, Instagram, and beyond
02

Content creation & production

Strategic content designed to be seen and shared — not filled in for the sake of posting.

  • Posts designed to be seen and shared, never just to tick a box
  • Short-form video and reels capturing school life — 2–3 shoot visits per term
  • Photography direction and editing, captions, and on-brand graphics
  • A consistent posting cadence so the channel never goes quiet
03

Community management & responsiveness

A school that listens and replies — present, warm, and always in control of the conversation.

  • Weekly monitoring and moderation of comments and messages
  • Warm, timely responses that show the school is present and listening
  • Flagging and escalation of anything sensitive, so you stay in control
  • Turning the channel into a genuine two-way line with families
04

Channel setup & optimisation

A profile that looks credible at first glance — and is built to be found.

  • Profile, bio, and page setup or refresh so the channel looks credible
  • Best-practice settings for reach, accessibility, and discoverability
05

Reporting & insights

Clarity at the end of every term — what worked, and exactly what to do next.

  • A term-end snapshot of growth, reach, and engagement
  • What worked, what to do more of, and recommendations for the month ahead
